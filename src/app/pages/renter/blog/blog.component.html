Da Nang International Terminal is certified to welcome Chinese passengers
Da Nang International Terminal (Terminal T2) is rated Gold - the highest of the 3 Silk, Jade, Gold grades of the Welcome Chinese certificate of China Tourism Academy, a Beijing-based research organization, established in 2008, under the National Tourism Administration of China. Da Nang International Terminal is the first in Southeast Asia to be granted the above certification. There are 11 criteria Danang International Terminal has met according to Welcome Chinese certification. The first is that the signage inside and outside the station is written in Chinese, in addition to Vietnamese, English, Korean.
