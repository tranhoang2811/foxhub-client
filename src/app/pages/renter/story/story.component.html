Who is FoxHub?

FoxHub was born with the vision of becoming the leading accommodation connection platform in Vietnam, contributing to promoting the development of the tourism industry and related services, positively impacting the domestic economy and bringing unique experience for tourists.

From the early days of forming the idea, FoxHub has always emphasized user experience in the process of using the service. FoxHub has set itself the mission of supporting tourists in their journey to find accommodation in Vietnam with the desire to improve image of the country's service industry in the eyes of international friends, while also acting as a means of stimulating tourism, encouraging domestic and foreign tourists to experience more forms of tourism combined with cultural exploration.

With that vision, FoxHub is committed to providing a variety of accommodation types, meeting the needs of target customers; ensure service quality of partners, promote customer satisfaction when using services; optimize your experience with the website.

Our Mission

"The platform connects experiences between lessors and lessees, generating revenue for the domestic economy, bringing unique travel experiences and contributing to promoting Vietnam's cultural image to international friends."

Our Vision

"Tourism approaches local cultural characteristics, aiming to enhance the user experience when traveling by establishing connections with local hosts, accessing each distinct culture in different locations where customers visit."
